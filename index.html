Retribution (Python partner project)

This is a short (currently unfinished) text-based adventure RPG where you earn your freedom by defeating a monster.

Nick and I started this project with a grand vision in our heads of a fully-fledged game with inventory, exploration, multiple choices, character interaction, and many monsters.

However, we quickly realized that we wouldn't have time to do all of this, so we decided upon the condensed version.

The hardest thing for this project was the battle system, which took a while to complete (and is still not finished).
